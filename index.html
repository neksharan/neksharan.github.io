Welcome!

I am a postdoctoral research associate in the Computational Physics and Methods group at the Los Alamos National Laboratory. Broadly, my research interests are in numerical methods/models for fluid-flow simulations, turbulent flow physics, mixing, combustion, high-performance computing and scientific machine learning.

Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher in GALCIT and Department of Mechanical and Civil Engineering at Caltech and doctoral researcher in the Department of Aerospace Engineering at UIUC.
